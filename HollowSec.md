Hello.  This document introduces **HollowSec**, a Spanish‑speaking community dedicated to cybersecurity education and mutual support.  Whether you are just starting your journey in ethical hacking or you are a seasoned professional looking to share knowledge, HollowSec provides a space to learn, collaborate, and grow – entirely in Spanish.

---

## What is HollowSec?

HollowSec is a growing community focused on **fostering education and support in the cybersecurity sector** for Spanish speakers.  The mission is simple: provide comprehensive resources to help members master both defensive and offensive security techniques, all within a supportive environment and in their native language.

From the website:

> *“Fomentando la educación y el apoyo en el sector de la Ciberseguridad. Utiliza nuestros recursos integrales para dominar el arte de la defensa y la ofensiva, y únete a una comunidad dedicada a crecer juntos.”*

---

## Core Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Education** | Resources, guides, and learning paths for all skill levels, all in Spanish. |
| **Support** | A place to ask questions, get help, and collaborate on challenges in your own language. |

---

## Why Join HollowSec?

- **Learn from peers** – Get help with labs, CTFs, and certifications, all explained in Spanish.
- **Share your knowledge** – Help others while reinforcing your own skills, in the language you are most comfortable with.
- **Stay updated** – Discuss the latest vulnerabilities, tools, and techniques with a Spanish‑speaking audience.
- **Network** – Connect with like‑minded individuals in the field from across the Spanish‑speaking world.

The community currently has **59 members online** (as of last check) and **279 total members**, indicating an active and engaged user base – all communicating in Spanish.

---

## How to Join

Joining HollowSec is simple:

1. **Visit the Discord invite:** [discord.gg/hollowsec](https://discord.gg/hollowsec)
2. **Create an account** (or log in if you already have one).
3. **Introduce yourself** in the appropriate channel and start participating.

> **Note:** HollowSec is a **Spanish‑only community**.  All conversations, resources, and support are in Spanish.  If you do not speak Spanish, we respectfully ask that you do not join, as this space is intended exclusively for Spanish speakers.

The Discord server requires JavaScript to run, and you will need to set a nickname and birth date during account creation (standard Discord procedure).

## A Note on Ethics

HollowSec is committed to **ethical hacking** and responsible disclosure.  The community exists to promote learning and defence, not malicious activity.  Members are expected to use their knowledge legally and ethically.

If this aligns with your values, you speak Spanish, and you are eager to grow in cybersecurity, HollowSec welcomes you.
